Our Story

About Little Lemon

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish cuisines, bringing the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean coast right to your plate.

Our Culinary Philosophy

We believe that dining is not just about food; it's about sharing a moment. That's why we source our ingredients locally and import our oils and cheeses directly from producers in Europe. Every dish is cooked with love, passion, and respect for heritage, according to time-tested recipes passed down through generations.

Meet Adrian & Mario

Brothers Adrian and Mario started Little Lemon with a simple dream: to create a warm, friendly neighborhood space where families could enjoy authentic, fresh, and healthy Mediterranean food. Today, they continue to run the kitchen and the dining floor, welcoming every guest as part of the family.
